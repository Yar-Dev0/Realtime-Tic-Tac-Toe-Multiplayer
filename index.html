How to Play

  • Real-Time Multiplayer: Two players compete using WebSockets.
  • Interactive UI: Modern interface with smooth animations.
  • Player Info: Displays symbols (X and O) and status updates.
  • Game Status: Shows current player turn and results (win, lose, or draw).
  • Restart Game: Players can restart after each match.
